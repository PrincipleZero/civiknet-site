FAQ

What can I use CivikNet Knowledge Support for?

CivikNet Knowledge Support covers all operational knowledge use cases. You can use it for staff onboarding, compliance training, procedure documentation, partner education, and beyond.

Can I brand the platform for a unique experience?

Absolutely. Apply your brand's colors, fonts, and logos for a customized look.

Is the authoring built-in?

Yes, CivikNet Knowledge Support has real-time collaborative authoring with powerful AI features to make document generation faster and more effective.

What types of content can we create and include?

CivikNet Knowledge Support supports blended content. You can create, host, and manage interactive procedures and live sessions from the same platform. You can also embed external content into your material. This includes videos, PDFs, and links from external tools. You can import content from external libraries.

Can I integrate CivikNet with my HRIS and CRM?

CivikNet has plug-and-play integrations with a range of CRM and HRIS systems. One of the main benefits of these integrations is automation. Explore all integrations here.

Can I automate enrollments?

Yes, you can set up automatic enrollment rules with CivikNet Knowledge Support’s Programs feature.

Can we migrate existing content from other knowledge management systems?

Yes, CivikNet's procedure generator transforms PDFs into interactive procedures, and you can import content from other knowledge management systems via SCORM.

What is CivikNet Knowledge Support’s pricing?

We tailor pricing to different business needs. You can find all information on the pricing here.

How secure will my company's knowledge and data be with CivikNet?

Very. We’re ISO 27001 certified, SOC 2 Type I and Type II certified, and GDPR compliant. Read more here: https://trust.sanalabs.com/
