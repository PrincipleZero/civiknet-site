Book intro

Public services that actually connect.

Public services were built one program at a time. Each program got its own forms, its own intake, its own data. So a family touching five agencies gets described five different ways. CivikNet fixes that. We give agencies one shared way to track the work. We measure whether families actually got help. And we listen to the people doing the work — so the system stays honest.

CivikAccess

The platform behind the Sprint. Built around the pathway — not the single case. Built around the workers on the ground — not the dashboard at the top. We route the work, not the family's identity. The platform knows what's happening. It does not know who it's happening to.

The Access Reliability Sprint

90 days. Fixed scope. Fixed price. We map your pathways with your team and your partners. We define who owns each handoff. We turn on the monitoring before we leave. By day 90, your team is working a new way — not just looking at a new tool.
